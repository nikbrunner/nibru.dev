Music

2009 was a time where i started to get interested in music production. It was the first area of interest that I pursued consistently and academically. With my growing knowledge and skills I became more ambitious. Although I was primarily interested in minimal techno, it was always my goal to integrate acoustic sounds or field recordings from the real world. After two years a label offered me a release contract and to this day my music can be found on Apple Music, Spotify, Amazon etc. under the name 'Shuqq'.
